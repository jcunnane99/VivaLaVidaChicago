Coldplay songs. Chicago energy.

Viva La Vida Chicago celebrates the music of Coldplay with a polished, energetic live show built for fans, families, clubs, festivals, private events, and community concerts.

The band performs the anthems and fan favorites people know and love, with a set that can include songs like “Yellow,” “Clocks,” “The Scientist,” “Fix You,” “Viva La Vida,” “Adventure of a Lifetime,” and “A Sky Full of Stars.”
